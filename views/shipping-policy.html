Shipping Policy

Delivery Time: Orders are typically delivered within 5-7 business days depending on your location.

Shipping Charges: Free shipping on orders above ₹999. Orders below that incur a flat ₹49 shipping charge.

Order Tracking: You can track your order status anytime from the "My Orders" page after logging in.

Delayed Deliveries: In rare cases (weather, courier delays), delivery may take longer. We'll notify you via email if there's a significant delay.

Areas Covered: We currently ship across India to all serviceable pincodes.
