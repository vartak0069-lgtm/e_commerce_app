Returns & Refunds

Return Window: Items can be returned within 7 days of delivery, provided they're unused and in original packaging.

How to Return: Go to "My Orders" → select the order → contact support with your order number to initiate a return.

Refund Timeline: Once we receive the returned item, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.

Non-Returnable Items: Certain items (perishables, personal care products) may not be eligible for return - this will be noted on the product page.

Questions? Reach out via support@mystore.com
